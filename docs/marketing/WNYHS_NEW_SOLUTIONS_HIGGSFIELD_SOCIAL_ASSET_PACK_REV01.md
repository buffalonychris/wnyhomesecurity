## 2. Shared production rules

- Structure: `Problem -> consequence -> WNYHS solution -> visual demonstration -> customer outcome -> CTA`.
- Tone: direct, professional, local, practical, non-technical, and non-alarmist.
- Show ordinary Western New York residential/property settings; do not show uniforms, third-party logos, medical scenes, fear imagery, property damage, active break-ins, or certainty text.
- Keep on-screen copy within mobile-safe margins and readable without audio.
- Use staged/generic homes, screens, and devices. Never show customer data, real addresses, private camera views, credentials, access codes, or identifiable customer records.
- Product appearance is illustrative unless the final approved scope names that exact product. Avoid logos and exact equipment close-ups by default.
- Final CTA on every asset: `Tell us what you want to solve. Request an on-site estimate.`
