# WNY Home Security QR Placard Production System — REV01

Status: Active / Canonical  
Context: CTX-WNYHS-FINISH-LINE-REV01  
Task ID: PRINT-ASSET001

## Purpose

This document governs WNY Home Security (WNYHS) QR placard production for physical local marketing placements. It defines the first deployable physical marketing asset system focused on scan-driven neighborhood visibility and local estimate generation.

The system is designed to keep QR placards:

- print-ready
- scan-reliable
- claims-safe
- brand-consistent
- easy to reproduce
- suitable for local placement
- visually aligned with approved WNYHS brand standards

## Placard Size Standards

Approved placard sizes:

1. **Half-page placard**
   - `8.5 x 5.5 in`
   - Useful for two-up letter-sheet printing
   - Primary near-term production size

2. **Full-page placard**
   - `8.5 x 11 in`
   - Useful for windows, counters, and larger bulletin boards

3. **Small counter card**
   - `4 x 6 in` or `5 x 7 in`
   - Optional later format
   - Must preserve QR scan reliability

4. **Outdoor laminated placard**
   - `8.5 x 11 in` preferred
   - Must use weather-resistant production or lamination

PRINT-ASSET001 prioritizes the half-page and full-page placard system first.

## QR Placement Rules

- QR should be visually prominent.
- QR must have quiet zone / white border.
- QR must not be placed over textured backgrounds.
- QR must not be smaller than `1.25 in` square.
- Preferred QR size for placards: `1.5–2.0 in` square.
- QR must have enough surrounding whitespace.
- QR must be tested after export and physical print.

## Indoor Placement Rules

Approved indoor placements:

- community boards
- shop counters
- diner/laundromat bulletin boards
- storefront windows where permitted
- local partner businesses
- waiting areas

Indoor production guidance:

- matte or satin paper preferred
- heavy cardstock preferred for hand placement
- lamination optional
- QR should be reachable and scannable at close range

## Outdoor Placement Rules

Approved outdoor use:

- temporary local placement where permitted
- protected exterior boards
- windows facing outward
- covered bulletin areas

Outdoor production guidance:

- laminate or weather-resistant print
- avoid glare-heavy gloss if QR scan reliability suffers
- protect QR from wrinkles, water, and obstruction
- phone/URL must remain readable if QR cannot be scanned

Do not imply permission to post illegally or on restricted property.

## Kinkos / Staples Ready Export Specs

- `8.5 x 11 in` full-page PDF export
- `8.5 x 11 in` two-up PDF for half-page placards
- `300 DPI` preferred
- export with bleed only if vendor layout requires it
- safe margin: minimum `0.25 in`
- QR must remain sharp
- embed images at sufficient resolution
- use PDF for print handoff
- preserve black/gold contrast
- proof before bulk printing

## Scan Testing Checklist

- [ ] QR scans from exported PDF on screen
- [ ] QR scans from vendor preview if available
- [ ] QR scans from first physical print
- [ ] QR scans under normal indoor lighting
- [ ] QR scans at expected viewing distance
- [ ] QR destination loads expected QR landing funnel (`https://www.wnyhomesecurity.com/qrlanding?src=placard` intent)
- [ ] Phone number is readable
- [ ] Fallback URL is readable
- [ ] No forbidden claims appear
- [ ] Print is readable in grayscale
