# QUOTE001 Customer Proposal Structure REV01

Status: PLACEHOLDER
Customer-facing: No
Implementation authority: No

## Purpose

This placeholder defines the future structure for a customer proposal package. It does not authorize customer quote issuance, pricing automation, payment changes, scheduling changes, or legal/compliance language for customer use.

## Section 1: Floorplan / Property Plan

The proposal should include the approved floorplan or property plan when available.

The plan should reference the approved base redraw and any relevant location labels used by the hardware plan.

## Section 2: Customer Goals And WNYHS Accommodation Plan

This section should include:

- Customer goals.
- How WNYHS will meet those goals.
- Additional practical uses enabled by proposed hardware.

## Section 3: Formal Business Quote

This section should include:

- Formal business quote.
- Explicit hardware list.
- Reference to floorplan and customer goals where relevant.
- 50% deposit required to schedule install.
- Remaining balance due upon arrival on scheduled install date.
- Scope assumptions.
- Exclusions.
- Change-order expectations.
- Legal/compliance review placeholder for New York business quote language.

## Review Boundary

Quote language must be reviewed before customer use. Do not claim final New York legal compliance without attorney or operator approval.
