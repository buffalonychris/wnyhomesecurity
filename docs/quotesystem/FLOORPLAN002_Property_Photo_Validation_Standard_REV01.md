# FLOORPLAN002 Property Photo Validation Standard REV01

Status: ACTIVE
Customer-facing: No
Implementation authority: No

## Purpose

This standard defines the role of exterior and interior photos in validating an approved Trace Mode floorplan redraw.

## Current Standard

Exterior and interior photos validate the floorplan after Trace Mode. Photos may identify conflicts, missing questions, or areas requiring operator review.

Photos do not override the operator/customer sketch unless an approved correction is documented.

Exterior photos are validation references only during trace mode. They can flag conflicts but must not silently override sketch geometry.

Compass orientation allows exterior photos to be mapped to sketch sides.

## Photo Validation Findings

Photo validation may identify:

- Missing or extra windows.
- Possible missing doors.
- Exterior projections.
- Garage openings.
- Likely camera or environment considerations.

During trace/redraw mode, conflicts must be documented as verification notes, not automatically corrected.

## Future Expansion

Future controlled passes should define:

- Photo-to-opening verification.
- Exterior side-to-opening reconciliation.
- Compass-aware camera and environment planning support.
- Interior photo evidence requirements by room type.
- Conflict logging and approval workflow.

## Boundary

This placeholder does not authorize photo analysis automation, hardware placement, floorplan generation, app changes, or runtime changes.
